Bucket List - Life is short, make it magical


Jimmy Cao

Jimmy Cao, a magician full of ambitions, has challenged himself to check off 52 bucket list items before turning 30. He has spent an entire year making his unique experience every week; From solving a rubik's cube to traveling to Japan! Join his magical show about his 52 bucket list items and why he decided to jump into this insane project. A magic show to add on your bucket list!

From Montréal, QC
